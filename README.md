### 📖 About Me

I am a graduate student in a teacher training program, majoring in **Computer Science and English**. My academic journey bridges the gap between analytical problem-solving and effective communication. I am passionate about exploring how technology and language intersect, and I strive to develop approaches that make complex concepts accessible, engaging, and impactful for future learners. 

Whether I am writing code, analyzing literature, or exploring new pedagogical methods, I am driven by a love for continuous learning and a desire to inspire others.
